How To Chicken Burger Ingredients: 2 buns, 1 tomato, 1 head of lettuce, 1 chicken patty, an oven, a piece of cheese, a microwave, a plate, a pan, a heat-proof glove, and a stove Step 1: Preheat oven to 375 degrees Fahrenheit and put the chicken patty in the oven for 25 mins. Step 2: Put pan over stove and turn on the part of the stove that the pan is on. Step 3: Put a little oil on the pan and let the pan heat for 1-2 minutes. Step 4: Place the 2 buns with the white face down on the pan and let them cook for 2 minutes. Step 5: Take the chicken patty out with the glove and take the buns out the buns out. Step 6: Put the cheese slice on the plate and heat it in the microwave for 15 seconds. Step 7: Take the cheese off the plate and put it in between the buns. Step 8: Put the chicken patty on the cheese and then put the lettuce and tomato on that.
